Content rules
Strategy · tone · structure · cadence
Post types
Insight post
2× per month · Week 1 + Week 3 Tuesday · Drawn from podcast transcripts or service expertise · Audience-first, no hard sell · Ends with a soft CTA
Offer post
1× per quarter · Q3 = July · Q4 = October · Lead with a business outcome · One service per post · Clear CTA: book a call or reply
Strategy
Goal is client acquisition
Every post should make a small business owner think "I need this." The podcast is a credibility engine — not the product being promoted.
Audience
Small business owners, founders, entrepreneurs. Pain points: no time, no team, need leads, need content, want to look professional online.
Tone
Peer-to-peer. Direct. No jargon. First-person. Founder talking to founder — not an agency pitching.
Post structure
Hook (stops the scroll) → 3–5 lines of insight → one takeaway → soft CTA. No walls of text.
